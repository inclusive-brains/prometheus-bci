About this interface

This first version of the Prometheus BCI platform was designed to allow Nathalie Labregere to interact with the exoskeleton arm and carry the Olympic flame on May 9, 2024, in Marseille. Due to her motor and cerebral disabilities, Nathalie could not use motor imagery-based commands; her sole means of interaction with the outside world is through her facial expressions.

The following sections enable you to monitor metric changes over time and adjust control settings by selecting a facial expression to integrate into the multimodal brain metric.
